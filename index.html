Who we are

From micro components to macro automation, we keep your plant running.

Spriha Associate is one of the leading industrial electronics equipment repairing centres and solution providers for industrial automation. We repair any type of industrial electronic board.

Using state-of-the-art equipment, our technicians and engineers troubleshoot problems down to the component level. We maintain a wide stock of replacement parts to ensure quick service, specialising in PCB repair that is fast, warranted and low-cost.

How a repair flows

Five steps from dead board to back online.

01

Receive & log

Your board arrives, is logged and matched to its make and model. Every unit gets a unique repair ticket.

02

Diagnose

We trace the fault to the component before any work begins. No guesswork — just lab-grade diagnostics.

03

Repair

Faulty components are replaced from our in-house stock with quality parts. SMD rework done with precision.

04

Test

The board is powered, signal-tested and verified against spec with our oscilloscopes and function generators.

05

Return

Repaired, warranted and delivered on time so your line gets back up and running with minimum downtime.
